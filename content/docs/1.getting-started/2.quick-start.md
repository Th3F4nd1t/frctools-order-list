# Quick Start

This guide will help you get started with FRCTools Orders in just a few minutes.

## Step 1: Create an Account

Navigate to [orders.frctools.com](https://orders.frctools.com/auth/signup) and create a new account:

1. Enter your **name**
2. Enter your **email address**
3. Create a **password**
4. Click **Sign Up**

::note
Your email will be used for account recovery and organization invitations.
::

## Step 2: Create Your Organization

After signing up, you'll be prompted to create your first organization:

1. Click **Create Organization**
2. Enter your **team name** (e.g., "FRC Team 1234")
3. Click **Create**

::tip
Use your FRC team number in the organization name to make it easy for team members to identify.
::

## Step 3: Invite Team Members

Once your organization is created, invite team members:

1. Go to the **Organization** page
2. Click **Invite Member**
3. Enter the **email address** of the team member
4. Select their **role** (Admin or Member)
5. Click **Send Invitation**

The invited member will receive an email with a link to join your organization.

## Step 4: Create Your First Order

Now you're ready to start tracking orders:

1. Click the **Add Order** button
2. Fill in the order details:
   - **Part Name** - The name of the part you need
   - **Description** - Optional notes about the part
   - **Quantity** - How many you need
   - **Tags** - Optional categorization
3. Click **Create**

::tip
Many popular FRC vendors have automatic information fetching! When you enter a vendor URL, FRCTools Orders will try to fill in details like part name and price for you.
::

Your order will be created with a "To Order" status.

## Step 5: Track Order Progress

As your order progresses, update its status:

- **To Order** - Part needs to be ordered
- **Ordered** - Part has been ordered, waiting for arrival
- **Arrived** - Part has arrived and is available

Simply drag orders between columns to change their status.
