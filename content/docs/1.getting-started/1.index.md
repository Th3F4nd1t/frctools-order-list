# Welcome to FRCTools Orders

FRCTools Orders is a simple and powerful order list application designed specifically for FRC (FIRST Robotics Competition) teams to manage their parts ordering workflow.

## What is FRCTools Orders?

FRCTools Orders helps FRC teams streamline their parts ordering process by providing a centralized platform where team members can:

- **Track orders** - Keep track of parts that need to be ordered, have been ordered, or have arrived
- **Collaborate** - Work together as a team with organization-based access control
- **Stay organized** - Use tags to categorize and filter orders

## Getting Started

Ready to get started? Follow these steps:

1. **Sign up** for an account at [orders.frctools.com](https://orders.frctools.com/auth/signup)
2. **Create an organization** for your FRC team
3. **Invite team members** to collaborate
4. **Start tracking orders** for your team
